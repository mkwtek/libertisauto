Frequently Asked Questions

We are located in Milpitas, CA (just outside of San Jose, CA) within walking distance to the Great Mall of the Bay Area, the VTA Light Rail "Orange Line", and the Milpitas Transit Center & BART Station. Our address is 1476 S Main St, Milpitas, CA 95035.

Our standard labor rate is competitive at $150/hr for exceptional service and expert diagnostics. Each situation is unique and estimates are provided prior to diagnostics or repair. No work will be performed without customer authorization.

We do not have a towing service but are a AAA approved Auto Repair facility just ask your tow truck company to drop your car off with us and give us a call prior drop off so we know to expect you and so that you have a clear understanding of possible costs and turn-around times.

We currently only schedule appointments over the phone. Please give us a call at (408) 263-3339.

If you have a classic, custom, or performance vehicle and need special care taken with your ride we pride ourselves on our service and attention to detail. Please contact us to discuss your individual needs, thank you.
